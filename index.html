📍 Location & Nearby Attractions

Andiambalama is a perfectly situated hub for travelers, offering a peaceful residential vibe while being just minutes away from Sri Lanka’s main gateway and vibrant urban centers.

Destination Travel Time Why Visit?
Bandaranaike International Airport (BIA) 5 Minutes Stress-free arrivals and departures. Ideal for long-stay travelers and transit guests.
Katunayake Town 5 Minutes Access to the Board of Investment (BOI) zone, major supermarkets, and quick transit links.
Minuwangoda Town 10 Minutes A bustling local hub for authentic Sri Lankan shopping, fresh produce markets, and local dining.
Negombo City & Beach 15 Minutes Famous for its golden sandy beaches, vibrant nightlife, seafood restaurants, and historic Dutch canals.

🍽️ Dining & Local Flavors

Whether you're looking for a quick bite, a cozy cafe, or a traditional Sri Lankan meal, there are plenty of options just a short drive away from Andiambalama.

🌟

Fine Dining

  • The Gateway Hotel (by Taj): 5 mins away. Perfect for a premium international buffet or dinner by the pool.
  • Lords Restaurant: 15-20 mins away. Amazing seafood, art gallery vibes, and live music.
  • Tusker Restaurant: High-quality dishes for a formal dining experience near the airport.
☕

Cafes & Quick Bites

  • Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf: Premium coffee, sandwiches, and pastries.
  • Java Lounge: Great ambiance, free WiFi, snacks and beverages in Negombo.
  • Perera & Sons / Keells: Quick Sri Lankan short eats and daily essentials.
🍛

Authentic Sri Lankan

  • Hela Bojun Hala: 100% authentic, fresh traditional food made by local women at very low prices.
  • Local Rice & Curry: Clean eateries in Andiambalama and Minuwangoda for village-style meals.
🍔

Fast Food Chains

Quick & familiar meals within 10-15 minutes:

  • Pizza Hut & KFC (Katunayake / Minuwangoda)
  • Burger King
  • McDonald’s (Located towards Negombo)
